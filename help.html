How can I add questions?

If you are an Instructor and you are new to this website you have to Signup first with your name and password.

And if you already signed up earlier you just have to Log in with your name and password. There you will find an option about saying if you want to take an exam? if your answer is yes,you can mark the yes option and submit it and then we will take you to the question adding page, now you can add questions here and easily take an exam.

How can I give a quiz test?

To give a quiz test you just have to Log in from Student portal. And in the place of password you need to enter the provided password from your instructor.After giving the password you can easily take your required test.
